# PRD Review

You are facilitating a structured multi-perspective review of a PRD. Review the PRD provided by the user against the template in `templates/ai_prd_template.md`.

Run the review from the perspective of each of the 5 personas below. For each persona, provide:
- **Strengths** (2-3 specific things done well)
- **Concerns** (2-3 specific gaps or risks)
- **Key Question** (1 question they'd ask before approving)

---

## Persona 1: Engineering Lead
Focus: Technical feasibility, clarity of functional and non-functional requirements, ambiguity in specs, AI/data requirements, and architecture.

## Persona 2: End User
Focus: Is the problem real and relatable? Does the solution actually help? Is the experience intuitive? Would a user trust and use this?

## Persona 3: Executive Sponsor
Focus: Strategic fit with company/team goals, ROI, risk exposure, timeline realism, and whether this is worth prioritizing now.

## Persona 4: Ethical AI Reviewer
Focus: Bias risks, data privacy, consent, unintended consequences for vulnerable user populations, and responsible AI use.

## Persona 5: Design Lead
Focus: Clarity of user journeys, quality of personas, UX considerations, accessibility, interaction design gaps, and whether the solution is designed around the user.

---

## Summary

After all 5 reviews, provide:

### Top 3 Things to Fix
The highest-priority gaps to address before this PRD is ready to share.

### Missing Sections
List any sections from `templates/ai_prd_template.md` that are incomplete or missing entirely.

### Overall Readiness
Rate the PRD on one of three levels:
- **Not Ready** — significant gaps, do not share yet
- **Needs Work** — solid foundation but key sections need attention
- **Ready to Share** — minor polish only, good to go
